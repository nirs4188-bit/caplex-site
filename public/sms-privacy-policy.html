SMS / Text Messaging Privacy Policy

Caplex Equity LLC  •  Last updated: June 23, 2026

This SMS Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Caplex Equity LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our text messaging (SMS/MMS) program. By providing your mobile phone number and agreeing to receive text messages from us, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Consent to Receive Messages

We send text messages only to individuals who have opted in to receive them. You provide express consent to receive SMS messages from Caplex Equity LLC when you join our Facebook group and agree to be added to our contact list, or otherwise affirmatively agree to receive text messages from us.

Consent to receive marketing or informational text messages is not a condition of any purchase or service.

2. Types of Messages We Send

The SMS program is used to send real estate deal alerts, including notifications about available investment properties, cash-buyer opportunities, and related real estate updates.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on available opportunities and deal flow.

4. Costs

Message and data rates may apply. These charges are billed by and payable to your mobile service provider. We are not responsible for any charges imposed by your carrier.

How to Opt Out (STOP): You can cancel the SMS service at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After you send STOP, we will send a one-time confirmation message, and you will not receive any further text messages unless you opt back in.

How to Get Help (HELP): Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us using the details in the Contact section below.

5. How We Use Your Information

We collect your mobile phone number and the consent record associated with it. We use this information solely to:

  • Deliver the text messages you have opted in to receive;
  • Respond to your requests, including HELP and STOP requests;
  • Maintain records of consent and opt-out as required by law; and
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our messaging program.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in data with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information may be shared only with the service providers (such as our SMS messaging platform) that help us operate and deliver the messaging program, and only to the extent necessary to provide those services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the agreed-upon purpose.

7. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect through the SMS program. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your phone number and consent records for as long as you remain opted in, and afterward only as long as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

9. Your Rights

You may opt out of the SMS program at any time by replying STOP. You may also contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued participation in the SMS program after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
